# Privacy Policy for PixelTime Watch Face

**Last updated:** March 8, 2026

## Overview

PixelTime ("the App") is a watch face application for Wear OS. Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains what data the App does and does not collect.

## Data Collection

**PixelTime does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data.**

The App does not:
- Collect personal information (name, email, phone number, etc.)
- Track your location
- Access your contacts, messages, or other apps
- Use analytics, advertising, or tracking services
- Transmit any data over the internet
- Store any data outside your device

## On-Device Data

The App reads the following data solely for display on your watch face. This data never leaves your device:
- **Current time and date** — displayed on the watch face
- **Complication data** (heart rate, step count, battery level, weather) — provided by Wear OS system data sources and displayed on the watch face. The App does not store or transmit this data.
- **User preferences** (selected color theme, time format, scanline toggle) — stored locally on your device by the Wear OS system.

## Third-Party Services

The App does not integrate with any third-party services, SDKs, or APIs.

## Children's Privacy

The App does not collect data from anyone, including children under 13.

If this policy is updated, the revised version will be posted here with an updated date.
